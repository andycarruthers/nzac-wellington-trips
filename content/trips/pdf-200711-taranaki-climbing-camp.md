Early Bird Registration ends December 1 Good alpine rock offers fantastic trad climbing within a brief walk of the camp. Go tramping in the Goblin Forest or alpine trekking with technical difficulty being anything you desire. The most spectacular backdrops ever encountered with views out to Ruapehu, the Kaikoura Ranges and the Tasman Sea.
