Sefton from the Douglas. Geoffroy Lamarche, John Nankervis and Wellywood exiles, Martin Hunter and Rob McBrearty had a few moist days in the Douglas in mid February. From an eyrie, high on the ledges above the Horace Walker icefall, they climbed the west ridge of Sefton, then walked out from Horace Walker Hut over the beautiful high route to Conical Hill Saddle and the Karangarua river. . John Nankervis
