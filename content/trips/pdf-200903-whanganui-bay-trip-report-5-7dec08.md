This was the biggest club trip of the year with 15 participants spending 2 primo days at "The Bay". Our first day at the Bay got off to a sobering start with the group spending the first hour or two trying to help in the helicoptering out of an injured climber.  If nothing else it was good to know that when things go wrong the local ambulance and rescue helicopter crews will be there in a flash to sort you out.  Best of luck in your recovery, Troy. Most of the focus was on the two extensive crags on the TR of The Bay = Whekenui and The Plateau.
