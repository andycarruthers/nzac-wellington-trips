# **The crusty couloir**

**A spring climb of Mount Cupola’s north face**  
Words and pictures Peter Laurenson  
  
Met Service had granted my wish – a two day weather window and an avalanche risk deemed green – low. Having been rebuffed twice before by Mount Cupola (the second time, just a few weeks earlier) due to bad weather and snow conditions, I was now a man on a mission.
