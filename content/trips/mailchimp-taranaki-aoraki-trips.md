## Upper Tasman Glacier

**Dates:** 15–24 October 2022

**Alp 1 Team:** Tom Wilson, Logan Olausen, Andrea Wolter

**Alp 2 Team:** Dan Waterston, Sam Brown, Mike Peszynski, Hamish Brown & Helen Lee

**Climbs completed:**

- Hochstetter Dome (2834m) — South/East Face ascent, North Ridge descent, Grade 2
- Mt Kain (2670m) — West Ridge ascent, Zero Politics descent, Grade 1+ (bolted abseil)
- Mt Abel (2688m) — Pencil Couloir ascent, Red Buttress descent, Grade 2+ (bolted abseil)

**Aborted climbs:** Elie De Beaumont, Mt Annan, Mt Sealy
