29 January 2005 11am start - $10 entry fee - Recreation, expert, youth and masters categories - BBQ and prize giving at the Rimutaka Forest Park afterwards - Sunday 30th January postponement day Check out www.powerband.org.nz or your local climbing shop/ gym for further information. NZAC Journals The club has had a set of NZAC journals donated by Dawn Everson to be sold and the proceeds to be donated to the Home of Mountaineering fund. The collection consists of every journal since 1935 to 2003 except for 1999. They are being sold by tender and you can tender for individual journals or the whole set. You should send your tender to Keith Moffat  e-mail moffat.k@xtra.co.nz or post to 172 Gladstone Rd Dunedin. Tenders close on 28th February 2005. Delivery to be paid by the purchaser.
