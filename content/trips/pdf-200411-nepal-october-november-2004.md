How about a slightly longer trip – to Nepal??  Several people have indicated interest in a section climbing trip to attempt several trekking peaks – Island Peak and Lubuje East have been suggested as reasonably straightforward objectives.  This could include a nonclimbing trek party also.  Trip leaders for both are emerging as discussions go on.
