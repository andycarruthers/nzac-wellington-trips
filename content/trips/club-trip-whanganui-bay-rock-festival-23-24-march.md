Join the Wellington crew at this year's rock fest. Whanganui Bay old timers and first timers welcome!

Climbing, chilling, crag workshops, connecting with the local community and fundraiser for Marae.

Leaving Wellington after work Friday 22 March, camping at the bay, returning Sunday 24 March.

Depending on numbers we may take a mini van so please RSVP asap.

**Contact Dan**: danielpringle75@gmail.com if you are interested or want some additional information about the trip.

NB: You will need to register for the Rock fest yourself online and pay $40 Rock fest fee (covers usual $20 camping plus Saturday dinner plus extras).

Or organize your own crew, register and see us up there!
