# **A Cold Night Out**

Lake Fohn Mount Aspiring National Park  
Words and photographs – Andy Carruthers  
  
This trip had been on my list for a few years. I had been on the Routeburn a few times recently and did a Rees Dart family tramp as a teenager.  
  
I had met a few folks who had done the route, all raved about the amazing country. After reading Geoff Spearpoints “The Great Unknown” a gift from my father in law I was inspired to reach deeper into the backcountry.
