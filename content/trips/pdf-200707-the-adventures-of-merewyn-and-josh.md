For the moment we are back in Calgary, more bike maintenance, we are now using 2 part epoxy to hold things together and stop them leaking.  The quick BC tour turned into an extended trip across BC and back though Washington, Idaho and Montana.  Lots of really great roads - with corners!  Josh has nearly worn the flat spot off his rear tire... or was that me when I was riding it? Lots of pics and the full story on the blog now: http://placebojim.blogspot.com In a few days we will head off again, think of us as we are crossing the great plains, boring boring boring.
