This last winter will be long remembered for the big snow cover in the eastern part of the South Island. I had great days off piste in the Craigieburns and Ohau. But it was the once in a decade cover that lured a swag  of us fading evergreens up to the glaciers of Ruapehu, weekend after weekend . An account of one memorable run follows. John Nankervis
