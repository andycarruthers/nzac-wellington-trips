|  **The 10th North Island Ski Touring Meet**  
Tukino on 2 - 4 October.   
Keith Munro  
  
Numbers continue to grow for this popular event, with 29 Wellington section members participating.  
Saturday was near bluebird with a gusty breeze. There was an initial boot pack to get to skinnable snow. Missions then headed all over. Jan, Graeme and I joined a few others in doing a number of laps in a gully that had ideal spring conditions at mid altitude as the top didn't soften.  
Sunday, the breeze was up a little with waterfalls flowing horizontally rather than vertically. Most headed out while a number of us bailed.  
Another great meet. Big thank you to Mac and Walter for organising the weekend and to Magnus and Anna for the avalanche awareness instruction.
